Terms & Conditions

ZipTask is a local task marketplace. Users are responsible for posting accurate task details, accepting only tasks they can perform, and completing agreed work lawfully and safely.

When a task is assigned, the task budget is locked from the creator wallet. When the creator marks the task complete, 95% is credited to the performer wallet and 5% is credited to the platform admin wallet.

A 5% platform fee is deducted from every completed transaction.

Users must not post illegal, unsafe, misleading, or abusive tasks. ZipTask may restrict accounts that misuse the platform or violate applicable law.

Wallet credits are processed through Razorpay and shown in INR. Users must maintain sufficient available balance before their posted task can be accepted.
