Privacy Policy

ZipTask collects account details, task details, wallet transactions, and payment references needed to run the marketplace. Passwords are stored only as secure hashes.

Payment processing is handled by Razorpay. ZipTask stores payment identifiers and transaction statuses, but does not store card, UPI, netbanking, or other sensitive payment instrument details.

We use your information to authenticate users, show tasks, process wallet credits, release task payments, maintain platform records, and comply with applicable legal obligations.

You can request account support or data correction by contacting the platform administrator.
